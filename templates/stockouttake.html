Stock Outtake

Use this form to record drugs leaving your inventory — whether dispensed, sold, or used. Selecting a drug and entering a quantity will reduce your stock count accordingly. If the full quantity is consumed, the entry is removed automatically.

Drug Selection The dropdown shows all drugs currently in stock along with their expiry dates — so you can confirm you're dispensing the right batch.
