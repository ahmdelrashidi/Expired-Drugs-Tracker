Expired Drugs

This page lists all drugs in your inventory that have passed their expiry date. These items must not be dispensed. Review this list regularly and move expired drugs to quarantine so they can be tracked until physically disposed of.

Expired From Shows how many days ago the drug expired. Drugs that have been expired longest should be prioritized for quarantine transfer.
Remove to Quarantine Clicking Remove moves the drug out of active stock and into the quarantine log. The drug is not deleted — it remains traceable until physically disposed of.
After Quarantine Once moved, the drug will appear in Stock Quarantine where it can be permanently removed after safe disposal.
