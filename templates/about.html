PharmaTrack

A pharmacy inventory system built to prevent expired drugs from reaching patients.

What is PharmaTrack?

PharmaTrack is a web-based inventory management tool designed for pharmacies and medical stockrooms. It tracks every drug in your inventory by name, quantity, and expiry date — and gives you clear visibility into what is expiring soon, what has already expired, and what needs to be disposed of safely.

The goal is simple: no expired drug should be dispensed to a patient because it was overlooked in a crowded stockroom.

How It Works

1
Add your stock Use Stock Intake to register every drug with its quantity and expiry date. Drugs expiring within 30 days are flagged immediately. Already-expired drugs are rejected at entry.
2
Monitor expiry windows Three expiry views — 6 months, 3 months, and 1 month — let you catch approaching expiry early and adjust dispensing or purchasing decisions before it becomes a problem.
3
Record dispensing Use Stock Outtake to reduce quantities as drugs are dispensed or sold. When a drug runs out completely, it is removed from inventory automatically.
4
Handle expired drugs Expired drugs are flagged in the Expired Drugs page. From there, move them to Stock Quarantine — a holding log for items awaiting safe physical disposal. Once disposed, remove them permanently from the system.

Pages at a Glance
